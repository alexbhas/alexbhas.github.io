About Me

Bio

Hello! I'm Alex, a fourth-year student in computer science at Carleton University. I am graduating in April 2023 and am looking for new graduate job opportunities.

The aspect of computer science that I am most interested is its potential applications. I have seen firsthand the impact well-designed websites and software can have on improving different areas of our lives. A long-standing goal of mine has been to contribute to developing applications that revolve around causes that are important to me. The foremost two of these causes are accessbility, and mental health awareness.

Personally, I have a wide variety of interests. I love listening to music, and could spend hours making playlists and discussing my favourite albums and artists. I enjoy learning and having new experiences, so I spend a lot of time reading about developments around the world, and participating in activities I find unique or fascinating.
